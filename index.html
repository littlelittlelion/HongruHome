Co-founder, GiftMe

11/2014-03/2015, Google, Dublin,Ireland.

GiftMe is a platform where you can ‘crowd-fund’ gifts that your friends will love. One of the biggest problems when buying presents for friends is not knowing what to buy. We have come up with an incredibly fun way to solve that problem. When you want to get something nice for your friend, just pick an item from their GiftMe wish list and start a crowd-funding campaign. If they don’t have a wish list, ask them to create one! Messages will be sent to all of your friends’ social network contacts so that anyone who knows your friend can chip in to buy that nice present. GiftMe takes care of collecting the money and purchasing the gift.
